## Permissions Explanation

The extension requests the following permissions solely for its core functionality:

### `storage`
Used to save your header configurations locally on your device. No data is transmitted externally.

### `declarativeNetRequest`
Required to modify HTTP headers according to your configuration. This is the core feature of the extension.

### `declarativeNetRequestFeedback`
Used to verify that your header modification rules are correctly applied.

### `<all_urls>` (Host Access)
Allows the extension to modify headers for websites you specify in your filters. The extension only acts on requests matching your configured rules.
